FOREIGN DIRECT INVESTMENT IN INDIAN DEFENCE SECTOR

1. CURRENT FDI LIMITS
India allows up to 74% FDI in defence manufacturing under automatic route.
FDI above 74% and up to 100% requires government approval.
Prior to 2020, the automatic route limit was 49%.
The liberalisation aims to attract global OEMs to manufacture in India.

2. CONDITIONS FOR FDI
Foreign companies must bring modern and state-of-the-art technology.
Products must have export potential.
Companies must comply with security conditions set by Ministry of Defence.
DPIIT (Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade) is the nodal authority.

3. INDUSTRIAL LICENSING
Defence manufacturing requires an industrial licence from DPIIT and MoD.
Licence is valid for 15 years, extendable by another 3 years.
Dual-use items may require separate licensing.
Small arms and ammunition have additional restrictions.

4. DEFENCE INDUSTRIAL CORRIDORS
Two Defence Industrial Corridors (DICs) established:
Uttar Pradesh DIC: Covers Agra, Aligarh, Lucknow, Kanpur, Chitrakoot, Jhansi.
Tamil Nadu DIC: Covers Chennai, Coimbatore, Salem, Tiruchirappalli, Hosur.
Target investment in each corridor: Rs 20,000 crore.
DICs offer single-window clearance and infrastructure support.

5. OFFSET LINKAGE TO FDI
Foreign companies with offset obligations under DAP 2020 can discharge offsets via FDI.
FDI in Indian defence companies counts as offset credit.
This creates a linkage between procurement and investment policy.

6. JOINT VENTURES
Many global OEMs have established JVs with Indian partners:
Tata-Lockheed Martin (F-16 wings), Mahindra-Airbus (helicopters), L&T-MBDA (missiles).
JVs enable technology access while meeting indigenisation requirements.
JV equity structure must comply with FDI limits.

7. EXPORT PROMOTION
India's defence export target: Rs 35,000 crore by 2025.
Actual exports in 2022-23: approximately Rs 16,000 crore.
Key export items: BrahMos missile system, Tejas aircraft (interest from Malaysia, Argentina), Akash system.
Export clearance requires MoD approval and End User Certificate from buyer country.
